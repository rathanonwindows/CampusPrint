Help & Support

How do I upload documents?
Go to the Upload page, select your document and place your order.
How do I track my order?
Enter your Order ID on the Track page to see status and queue.
Where do I collect prints?
Collect from the stationery shop once it shows "Ready".
Payment methods?
Cash or QR payment available.
How long does it take?
Depends on queue. Estimated time is shown in the system.
